## App Name
Nudge - App Blocker

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Break phone addiction. Delay-to-open, breathing exercises, time limits.

## Full Description

Your phone is designed to be addictive. Every app, every notification, every infinite scroll is engineered to steal your attention.

Nudge fights back.

Instead of blocking apps outright (which never works long-term), Nudge creates a moment of friction. A pause. A breath. Just enough time for your brain to ask: "Do I actually need this right now?"

That pause changes everything.


HOW IT WORKS

When you open a blocked app, Nudge intercepts it and shows a countdown screen with a rotating motivational message. You can wait it out or tap "I changed my mind" and walk away. Most people walk away.

You choose the intervention per app:

  - Delay-to-Open: 5 to 60 second countdown with motivational messages
  - Breathing Exercise: Guided breathe-in, breathe-out before opening
  - Hard Block: Completely prevent opening the app
  - Time Budget: Allow X minutes per day, then block
  - Schedule: Block during work hours, allow on weekends
  - In-App Feature Block: Block YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, or TikTok without blocking the whole app


WHY NUDGE IS DIFFERENT

Most app blockers are made by data companies. They use Accessibility Services to monitor every app you open, every screen you view, every word you type. Then they send that data to their servers for "analytics."

Nudge has zero internet permission. It physically cannot connect to the internet. Your usage data never leaves your device. Not to our servers. Not to anyone's servers. We don't have servers.

Check our AndroidManifest.xml yourself. Zero network permissions. Zero tracking. Zero telemetry.


FEATURES

  - Delay-to-open with countdown timer (5s / 15s / 30s / 60s)
  - Rotating motivational messages during countdown
  - Guided breathing exercises before opening distracting apps
  - Hard blocking to completely prevent app access
  - Per-app daily time budgets

  - In-app feature blocking (YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok)

  - Grayscale mode to make your phone less appealing
  - Dashboard with stats: blocks, times walked away, screen time
  - Material You design with warm, calming interface


BUILT ON SCIENCE

Nudge is based on "nudge theory" from behavioral economics. Small changes in how choices are presented can dramatically change behavior without restricting freedom. A 15-second delay doesn't prevent you from using Instagram. But it gives your prefrontal cortex time to override the dopamine-seeking impulse.

Research shows that adding even small friction to habitual behaviors reduces them by 20-40%.
